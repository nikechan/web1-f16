Homework #7

  • Course Home

Tasks

  1. Student page: Add a large screen responsive breakpoint to your student page

    • At the very bottom of your CSS file add: 
      /* large-screen responsive breakpoint */
@media screen and (min-width: 33em) {
  /* we color h1 red just so
     we know this break-point is working */
  h1 { color: red; }
}
    • Add 1 or more CSS properties in your large-screen breakpoint. You may delete the color: red; line.
    • Take 1 screenshot (or photograph) of your styled page on a phone, tablet or other small screen
    • Take 1 screenshot (or photograph) of your styled page on a large screen like a laptop or iMac
    • Show your screenshots as <img>s on your student page

  2. Final project: Carefully prepare at least 1 JPG image for your final project.

    • Read chapter 16 on images (from HTML & CSS)
    • Add at least 1 well prepared <img> to your final project page. Your primary src image should be less than 100kb

      in the html file:

        <img class="portfolio-image" 
  src="boxes-600px.jpg 
  alt="several cardboard boxes">

      and in your CSS file, add something like

        .portfolio-image {
    max-width: 200px;
  }
