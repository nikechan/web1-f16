Homework #8

  • Course Home

Tasks: Final Project

  1. HTML for final project

    • Finalize (as much as possible) the HTML on your homepage.
    • Add all remaining HTML pages (if you have a multi-page site). They do not have to be fully populated with your final content (image/text).
    • Verify all of your navigation links work.
    • Pages do not have to have any CSS styling.

  2. Images

    • Continue to prepare image assets as JPGs (or PNG) for your final project site. Your images should be less than 100kb each. If you are building a portfolio website aim to have at least 5 working images.
    • Include your images as <img> elements in your HTML pages. Verify all images display correctly.

  3. Screen Reader Testing

    • Test your html pages using a screenreader. Either VoiceOver on your Mac, or VoiceOver on your iPhone, or any other screen reader.

Revisions
