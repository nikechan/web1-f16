Homework #6 —Screen Reading

  • Course Home

Verify your Event Promotion page is usable using a screen reader of your choosing. Due by the start of next class.

Goals

  1. Practice using a screen reader to gain confidence testing with it on future projects.
  2. Gain an understanding of how one can change HTML to ease screen reader use.

Deliverables

  1. Create a list of use cases for your event promotion page.
  2. Using a screen reader attempt to achieve this use case. Document the steps you took including what you listened to and what key-presses were required.
  3. Take a look at the list you have created. Are there too many steps? Confusing steps? Is the use case even achievable? Update your HTML to resolve issues.
  4. Using a screen reader attempt again to achieve this use case. Document again.
  5. Record all of your documentations on your Student Page
